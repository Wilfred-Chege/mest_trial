About Zendawa

Neighborhood pharmacies account for 66% of primary healthcare providers but are not equipped for last mile service delivery, struggling with access to working capital, managing cash flow and inventory, and last-mile delivery to end users.

Zendawa is a B2B SaaS company building an end-to-end digital platform offering embedded finance, digital business management tools, and tele-pharmacy enabling neighborhood pharmacies to sell online and offer e-consultation.
