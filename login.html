About

Meet Wilfred Chege, a dynamic and visionary 2x Tech founder hailing from the vibrant city of Nakuru, Kenya. His journey into the world of technology began at Nakuru High School, where he developed a deep fascination for computers and innovation. Fuelled by this passion, he embarked on his higher education journey at Jomo Kenyatta University, where he honed his skills and knowledge in the field of technology. Driven by a profound desire to make a meaningful impact on his continent, Wilfred Chege has dedicated his career to leveraging technology as a force for positive change in Africa. With a keen understanding of the unique challenges faced by the African people, he founded his first tech startup,Shulemall, which aimed to address critical issues such as access to education and healthcare through innovative digital solutions. Building on the success of his first venture, Wilfred Chege went on to establish Zendawa, where he continues to work tirelessly to develop cutting-edge technological solutions that empower Africans and uplift communities across the continent. His relentless commitment to innovation and his unwavering belief in the transformative power of technology make him a true trailblazer in the Kenyan and African tech landscape. Wilfred Chege is a shining example of the incredible potential that lies within Africa's tech ecosystem, and he remains an inspiration to aspiring entrepreneurs and technologists across the continent.
